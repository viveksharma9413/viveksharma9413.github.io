About Me — Vivek Sharma

I’m a Senior Data Engineer with close to 10 years of experience building, optimizing, and scaling robust data platforms. I specialize in real-time data pipelines, data lake architecture, event-driven ingestion systems, and big data automation, with a proven track record of transforming business-critical infrastructure across healthcare, fintech, AdTech, and SaaS verticals.


Throughout my career, I’ve bridged the gap between hands-on engineering and strategic leadership. From designing ETL frameworks and compliance-ready dataflows to mentoring team members and aligning with cross-functional stakeholders, I’ve consistently driven outcomes that scale with business growth and technical complexity.

What I Do Best

  • 🔧 Architect cloud-native, event-driven data platforms using GCP, AWS, Spark, BigQuery, Kafka, Kinesis, and Databricks.
  • 🤝 Lead cross-functional projects, aligning engineering goals with compliance, analytics, and product requirements.
  • 🧬 Deliver scalable systems with config-driven designs, ensuring long-term maintainability and adaptability.
  • 🧑‍🏫 Mentor engineers and own core data tooling that serves as the backbone of an organization’s data ecosystem.

Philosophy & Leadership

I believe in building scalable systems and scalable people. Every platform I’ve architected is built to adapt, evolve, and empower. I treat platform resilience, team onboarding, and cross-functional alignment as first-class citizens of engineering success.


I’ve operated as a technical lead long before earning the title — taking ownership of platform vision, engaging in sprint planning, writing design docs, and mentoring junior engineers on best practices and patterns.

What’s Next

I’m on a path toward an official Engineering Manager or Data Architect role — one where I can drive systemic change while enabling my team to do their best work. I want to keep solving hard data problems, building tools that matter, and helping companies turn their data into a durable strategic advantage.
